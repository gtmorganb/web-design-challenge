Purpose of Project

This website was created in order to give me, the creator, practice making a website AND to show you, the consumer, meaningful weather data. There are four pages dedicated to visualize and explain weather data for cloudiness, humidity, temperature, and wind speed. The weather data presented here was collected on November 22, 2021. While exploring the site, you can make it back to this home page by clicking "Weather Data" in the top left corner. To see all the data at once in scatter plot form, click on "Data Comparisons" and to see the data in a table click on "Data." If you hover over, "Plots," you will see each of the scatter plots for your choosing. All of the plots are related to latitude. You can look at cloudiness, humidity, temperature, and wind speed.
