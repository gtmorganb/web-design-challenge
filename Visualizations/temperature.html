Temeprature Data


The scatter plot shown displays data points that corresspond to changing latitudes and temperature values. The temperatures shown are in fahrenheit. The plot shows temperatures increase from -40 degrees to about 0 degrees latitude. Starting at 0 degrees and going postive, the temperatures start to drop again. This makes sense with knowing that 0 degrees latitude is at the equator. So, temperatures are warmest at the equator and drop as latitude moves farther from zero.
