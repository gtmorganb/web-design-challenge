Cloudiness Data


The scatter plot shown displays data points that corresspond to changing latitudes and cloudiness values. These values were found on November 22, 2021. Based on this plot, there does not look to be a clear trend between cloudiness and latitude. However, there are several degrees of latitude that have higher densities of clousiness values. Between -20 and -40 degrees latitude, cloudiness has several data points at 0. Between -40 degrees latitude and 10 degrees latitude, there is a high concenttration of 100% cloudiness. Between 15-50 degrees latutude, there is a concentration of 0% cloudiness.
